How to use:

1.Drag the prefab into the object camera
2.Make sure the camera is Tagged as "MainCamera"
3.Adjust the Z axis of the scaler component until it match your size requirements
4.Feel free to adjust the script as you please

Enable Opaque Texture in URP Settings:

1.Go to your URP Asset (Edit > Project Settings > Graphics > Scriptable Render Pipeline Settings).

2.Select your URP asset and find the Opaque Texture checkbo and enable it.

Enable Opaque Texture on the Camera:

Select your Main Camera in the scene.

In the Rendering section, make sure Opaque Texture is also enabled.
